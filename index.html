Where it all began

We are the Ubuyashiki family. We are responsible for ending the age of demons. Demons are heartless creatures that are only trying to devour people.

The Ubuyashiki family was cursed a long time ago by a man called Muzan Kibutsuji. This curse makes it impossible for our members to live long, and it has been like that for thousands of years.

The only way to save us from this harm is by killing the monster who cursed us in the first place. This monster is Muzan, who was and is the first demon until today. Since this tragedy destroyed our family, we are trying to catch Muzan. But the only way to do so is by letting other children help, and we are fortunate to have many children.
